Instructions

Press the START button to begin the game.

The colours on the smaller grid/dice will be mixed.

A countdown clock will be started.

Your challenge is to match the 9 inner tiles on the larger grid to the 9 colours on the dice.

Click or press a tile to move it.

Only a tile beside an empty space can be moved.
